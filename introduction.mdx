# Introduction to Defensoría Civil Divorce Platform

The **Defensoría Civil divorce platform** is an automated legal assistance system designed to streamline divorce proceedings in San Rafael, Mendoza, Argentina. Built with **Clean Architecture** principles and powered by advanced **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, the platform provides an intelligent, conversational experience through WhatsApp.

## Platform Overview

This system transforms the traditionally complex and paper-heavy divorce filing process into a guided, conversational workflow that citizens can complete entirely via WhatsApp. The platform collects necessary information, validates data in real-time, processes legal documents using OCR, and generates properly formatted legal petitions.

## Key Stakeholders

The platform serves two primary stakeholder groups:

### Citizens Seeking Divorce

**Target Users**: Adults (18+) in San Rafael, Mendoza who need to initiate divorce proceedings but may face barriers such as:
- Limited legal knowledge
- Time constraints for in-person office visits
- Difficulty understanding complex legal terminology
- Uncertainty about required documentation

**User Experience**: Citizens interact with the system entirely through WhatsApp, receiving:
- Step-by-step guidance through the divorce filing process
- Real-time validation of provided information
- Clear explanations of requirements in conversational Spanish (Argentine dialect)
- Automated document processing and generation

### Defensoría Civil Operators

**Target Users**: Legal operators and administrators at the Defensoría Civil office responsible for:
- Reviewing submitted cases
- Validating citizen-provided information
- Managing case workflows
- Generating legal documents

**User Experience**: Operators access a web-based dashboard to:
- Monitor incoming cases and their completion status
- Review collected information and uploaded documents
- Track system metrics and performance
- Manage user accounts and permissions

## Problem Statement

Divorce proceedings in Argentina traditionally require:
1. **Multiple in-person visits** to legal assistance offices during business hours
2. **Paper-based forms** with complex legal terminology
3. **Manual document collection** and validation
4. **Extended processing times** due to incomplete submissions
5. **High barrier to entry** for citizens with limited legal knowledge

These challenges create significant friction, especially for:
- Working individuals who cannot easily visit offices during business hours
- Citizens in rural areas with limited access to legal services
- People with lower education levels who struggle with legal jargon
- Cases involving sensitive situations (e.g., domestic violence) where multiple in-person visits create emotional burden

## Solution Overview

The Defensoría Civil platform addresses these challenges through:

<Steps>
  <Step title="WhatsApp-Based Interface">
    Citizens initiate and complete the entire process via WhatsApp, the most popular messaging platform in Argentina. No app installation or special software required.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Conversational Data Collection">
    An AI-powered chatbot guides users through data collection using natural, conversational language rather than legal jargon. The system maintains context across the entire conversation and remembers previous interactions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Intelligent Validation">
    Real-time validation ensures data quality:
    - Age verification (minimum 18 years for marriage)
    - Date sequence validation (birth → marriage → separation)
    - Address validation for San Rafael/Mendoza jurisdiction
    - Document authenticity checks via OCR
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automated Document Processing">
    Users send photos of documents (DNI, marriage certificates) directly through WhatsApp. Gemini Vision OCR extracts structured data automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Smart Memory System">
    Four-layer memory architecture:
    - **Immediate**: Last 10 messages for conversation flow
    - **Session**: Current case data
    - **Episodic**: Historical conversation summaries with semantic search
    - **Semantic**: Legal knowledge base for domain-specific guidance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Safety & Security">
    Multi-layer security:
    - Hallucination detection (>99% accuracy) prevents AI from inventing facts
    - Prompt injection protection filters malicious inputs
    - Rate limiting (30 req/min per IP, 100 req/min per user)
    - Security headers (CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options)
  </Step>
</Steps>

The platform is built on modern, production-grade technologies:

## Architectural Principles

The system follows **Clean Architecture** (Uncle Bob Martin) with strict layer separation:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│        Presentation Layer               │
│  (FastAPI routes, webhooks, CLI)        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                  ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│       Application Layer                 │
│  (Use cases, services, DTOs)            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                  ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│     Infrastructure Layer                │
│  (DB, AI clients, messaging, OCR)       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                  ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│          Domain Layer                   │
│    (Entities, business rules)           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Key Principles**:
- **Dependency Inversion**: All dependencies point inward toward the domain
- **Interface Segregation**: Small, focused interfaces defined in Application layer
- **Single Responsibility**: Each class has one reason to change
- **Open/Closed**: Extensible without modifying existing code (e.g., LLM fallback system)
